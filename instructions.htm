HEXATIC ROOM      

 

 
<< Back

This online software employs Monte Carlo minimization to determine an optimal arrangement of persons in a crowded environment to maximize the distance between them. This can be useful to optimize space and resources in the current Coronavirus pandemic.

The steps to analyze the room or space of interest are:
  1. Load the plan containing the area, space or room of interest as an image (png or jpeg).
  2. Select an area of interest by selecting the Room radio button. This allows to draw a blue rectangle on the canvas region limiting the allowed area for thethe location of persons. You can draw more than one room but only the room drawn in the last step will be analyzed. If you want to analyze multiple areas on the same plan, just follow all the steps from 1 to 7 and then repeat the process by drawing a new room.
  3. If desired, include restricted areas on the analysis. This impedes positioning individuals on indicated areas. For instance, restraints can be useful to incorporate the limitations impsoed by architectonic elements, furniture or any other element not permitting the positioning of persons on the given area. Select the Restraints radio button and draw as many restraints as desired. Restraints are indicated as red rectangles.
  4. Assign an scale to plan. Select the Scale radio button and draw a line on the plan with known dimensions attending to the scale of the plan. Enter the number of meters corresponding to the width of the line drawn on the Scale numerical field. This sets a conversion factor between pixels on the image and meters.
  5. Indicate the number of individuals to position on the #Individuals numerical field. (Note that one can put as many persons as desired, but a 2 meters separation is, obviously, not granted).
  6. Click on the Analysis button to determine an optimal positioning of persons. This can take some time depending on the number of persons and the area selected.The Log console shows the distance between individuals attained and a reasonable estimation of the maximum number of persons necessary to keep a 2 meters separation between them.
  7. Download the image with the resulting arrangement by clicking on the Download button. Due to the stochastic nature of the procedure it is convenient to run multiple analysis, saving the different configuration and selecting the more convenient for the particular user purposes
